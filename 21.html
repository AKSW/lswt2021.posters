Semi-automatische Abfrage und Verknüpfung offener bibliografischer Daten als ergänzendes Feature eines Digitalen Modulkatalogs

Philipp Bolte

Implementation of a literature search for the semi-automated maintenance of the existing knowledge graph of the digital module catalog at the Technical University of Brandenburg. The implementation was carried out by students as part of the EKG21 module on the basis of the existing research project on the digital module catalog.
